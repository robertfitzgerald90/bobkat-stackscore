# Purpose

The BobKat Technology Improvement Lifecycle (BTIL) defines the standardized consulting methodology used throughout every client engagement.

It establishes the repeatable process that guides clients from initial discovery through long-term technology improvement.

All modules within BobKat StackScore are designed to support one or more phases of this lifecycle.

---

# Overview

Every client engagement follows five phases:

Discover

↓

Assess

↓

Plan

↓

Implement

↓

Measure

↓

Repeat

The lifecycle is continuous rather than linear.

Each completed reassessment becomes the starting point for the next improvement cycle.

---

# Phase 1 – Discover

## Purpose

Develop a foundational understanding of the client's business, technology environment, goals, and operational challenges.

This phase establishes the context required for an accurate Technology Profile.

---

## Inputs

* Client information
* Business overview
* Industry
* Number of users
* Number of locations
* Existing infrastructure
* Current technology challenges
* Business goals
* Future growth plans

---

## Outputs

* Client Profile
* Discovery Notes
* Initial Business Context

---

## StackScore Modules

* Clients
* Discovery Worksheet

---

## Business Outcome

BobKat IT gains a clear understanding of the client's environment before making recommendations.

---

# Phase 2 – Assess

* StackScore

* Improvement Opportunities

* Service Bundles

## Outputs

* Projected StackScore

---

# Continuous Improvement

Technology management is an ongoing partnership rather than a one-time project.

Each reassessment begins a new improvement cycle.

The Technology Profile remains a living document that evolves alongside the client's business.

---

# Lifecycle Rules

The following rules apply throughout the lifecycle.

* Every client begins with Discover.
* Every client completes a StackScore Assessment before recommendations are generated.
* Every Improvement Opportunity maps to one or more services.
* Every Technology Roadmap is generated from the Technology Profile.
* Every proposal is generated from an approved Technology Roadmap.
* Every approved proposal can generate one or more projects.
* Every completed project contributes to future reassessments.
* Every reassessment compares against the previous Technology Profile.
* Every completed lifecycle should demonstrate measurable business improvement.

---

# Success Criteria

The Technology Improvement Lifecycle is successful when:

* Every engagement follows a consistent methodology.
* Technology Profiles improve over time.
* Clients clearly understand both current and future technology maturity.
* Recommendations become completed projects.
* Completed projects produce measurable improvements.
* BobKat IT delivers a consistent consulting experience across every client.
