# Purpose

This document defines the long-term vision for BobKat StackScore.

It serves as the guiding document for all future development and establishes why the platform exists, who it serves, and the principles that drive product decisions.

---

# Mission

BobKat StackScore exists to help organizations understand, improve, and continuously measure the health of their technology through a consistent, repeatable, and executive-friendly process.

Rather than simply identifying technical issues, StackScore transforms technology assessments into measurable Technology Profiles, strategic roadmaps, and actionable improvement plans that help organizations become more secure, resilient, and efficient over time.

StackScore does not measure technology maturity.

StackScore manages the entire technology lifecycle of an organization—from initial assessment, through strategic planning, phased investment, implementation, measurement, and continuous improvement.

---

# Vision

To become the central operating platform for BobKat IT, providing a standardized methodology for technology consulting, strategic planning, project execution, and continuous technology improvement.

Every client engagement should follow the same professional lifecycle while remaining flexible enough to adapt to the unique needs of each organization.

---

# Product Purpose

StackScore exists to eliminate inconsistency in technology consulting.

Instead of relying on handwritten notes, spreadsheets, or consultant experience alone, every engagement follows a documented methodology that produces measurable outcomes.

The platform captures information once and intelligently reuses it throughout the client lifecycle.

---

# Primary Objectives

* Standardize technology assessments.
* Build a living Technology Profile for every client.
* Generate executive-friendly reports.
* Prioritize Improvement Opportunities.
* Produce Technology Roadmaps.
* Convert approved work into projects.
* Measure progress through reassessments.
* Demonstrate measurable business value over time.

---

# Target Users

## Primary

Small and medium-sized businesses seeking strategic technology guidance and managed IT services.

## Secondary

Residential customers who benefit from the same structured assessment and project workflow.

## Internal

BobKat IT consultants using StackScore to deliver a consistent consulting experience.

---

# Definition of Success

StackScore is successful when it enables BobKat IT to:

* Complete a full assessment during a client visit.
* Generate an executive-ready report before leaving the site.
* Produce a technology roadmap with estimated investment in real time.
* Convert approved recommendations into projects without duplicate data entry.
* Demonstrate measurable improvement through quarterly reassessments.
* Deliver a consistent consulting methodology across every engagement.

---

# Long-Term Vision

StackScore will become the operational foundation of BobKat IT.

Every engagement—from initial discovery through long-term technology planning—will be managed through a single, standardized workflow that emphasizes business outcomes over technical complexity.

The platform will continue evolving, but its core mission will remain unchanged:

**Help organizations continuously improve the health of their technology.**
