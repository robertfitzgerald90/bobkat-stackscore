# DOC-151G – Documentation & Knowledge
## Purpose
This document defines the Documentation & Knowledge assessment standard within the StackScore Technology Maturity Framework.
## Business Question
> Could another trusted IT professional successfully support this environment tomorrow?
## Desired Outcome
A mature documentation & knowledge capability that supports secure, reliable business operations.
## Assessment Philosophy
Assess conversationally, remain vendor agnostic, and focus on business outcomes.
