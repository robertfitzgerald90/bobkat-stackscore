# DOC-151B – Endpoint Management
## Purpose
This document defines the Endpoint Management assessment standard within the StackScore Technology Maturity Framework.
## Business Question
> Are company devices secure, standardized, and consistently managed?
## Desired Outcome
A mature endpoint management capability that supports secure, reliable business operations.
## Assessment Philosophy
Assess conversationally, remain vendor agnostic, and focus on business outcomes.
