BobKat StackScore - Assessment Categories

Purpose
BobKat StackScore is designed to provide small and medium-sized businesses with a clear understanding of their technology maturity, security posture, operational resilience, and overall IT health.

The platform evaluates technology environments using standardized assessment criteria and produces actionable recommendations that reduce business risk, improve reliability, and support long-term growth.
The primary goal is not to score technology itself, but to measure how well technology supports business operations.
________________________________________
Scoring Framework

Total Score: 100 Points
The overall StackScore is calculated from seven primary categories.
________________________________________
1. Security & Protection (20 Points)

Measures the organization's ability to protect systems, users, and data from cybersecurity threats.

Areas Assessed
•	Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
•	Endpoint Protection
•	Email Security
•	Patch Management
•	Password Policies
•	Security Awareness Training
•	Administrative Access Controls
•	Vulnerability Management

Business Impact
A weak security score increases the likelihood of ransomware, account compromise, business interruption, and data breaches.
________________________________________

2. Backup & Recovery (20 Points)
Measures the organization's ability to recover from data loss, ransomware, hardware failure, or accidental deletion.

Areas Assessed
•	Server Backups
•	Workstation Backups
•	Microsoft 365 Backups
•	Backup Retention Policies
•	Backup Monitoring
•	Backup Testing
•	Recovery Documentation
•	Offsite Storage
Business Impact
A weak backup score increases the likelihood of permanent data loss and extended downtime following an incident.
________________________________________
3. Infrastructure & Network Health (15 Points)
Measures the reliability and lifecycle health of core technology infrastructure.

Areas Assessed
•	Firewall Health
•	Switch Lifecycle
•	Wireless Coverage
•	ISP Reliability
•	Redundancy
•	UPS Protection
•	Network Segmentation
•	Hardware Age

Business Impact
A weak infrastructure score increases outages, connectivity issues, and operational disruption.
________________________________________
4. Endpoint & Asset Management (15 Points)
Measures visibility and management of company-owned devices.


Areas Assessed
•	Device Inventory
•	Centralized Management
•	Warranty Tracking
•	Asset Lifecycle Management
•	Endpoint Monitoring
•	Remote Support Capability
•	Operating System Currency

Business Impact
A weak endpoint score increases support costs, security risks, and operational inefficiencies.
________________________________________
5. Documentation & Operational Readiness (10 Points)
Measures how well technology knowledge is documented and maintained.

Areas Assessed
•	Network Diagrams
•	Vendor Contacts
•	Administrative Credentials
•	Standard Operating Procedures
•	Onboarding Documentation
•	Offboarding Procedures
•	IT Inventory Documentation

Business Impact
A weak documentation score increases recovery times and creates dependency on specific individuals.
________________________________________
6. Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery (10 Points)
Measures the organization's preparedness for major business disruptions.

Areas Assessed
•	Disaster Recovery Plans
•	Recovery Objectives
•	Emergency Contacts
•	Remote Work Readiness
•	Generator Availability
•	Recovery Testing
•	Hurricane/Natural Disaster Planning

Business Impact
A weak continuity score increases downtime and operational disruption during emergencies.
________________________________________
7. Strategic Technology Management (10 Points)
Measures whether technology is being managed proactively rather than reactively.

Areas Assessed
•	Technology Roadmaps
•	Budget Planning
•	Hardware Refresh Cycles
•	Software Lifecycle Planning
•	Compliance Readiness
•	Business Alignment
•	Strategic Reviews

Business Impact
A weak strategic score often results in technical debt, unexpected expenses, and stalled business growth.
________________________________________
Score Ratings

90-100
Exceptional
Technology is mature, secure, documented, and strategically managed.

80-89
Strong
Minor improvements recommended but overall risk remains low.

70-79
Stable
Several improvements should be prioritized within the next 12 months.


60-69
At Risk
Multiple weaknesses exist that could negatively impact operations.

Below 60
Critical
Significant technology risks exist that require immediate attention.
________________________________________
Core Principle
The purpose of StackScore is not to create technical reports for IT professionals.
The purpose of StackScore is to translate technology risk into business risk and provide a roadmap for improvement.
