# DOC-151C – Network & Connectivity
## Purpose
This document defines the Network & Connectivity assessment standard within the StackScore Technology Maturity Framework.
## Business Question
> Is the network secure, reliable, and built to support business operations?
## Desired Outcome
A mature network & connectivity capability that supports secure, reliable business operations.
## Assessment Philosophy
Assess conversationally, remain vendor agnostic, and focus on business outcomes.
