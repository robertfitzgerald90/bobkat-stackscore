# DOC-151H – Technology Strategy
## Purpose
This document defines the Technology Strategy assessment standard within the StackScore Technology Maturity Framework.
## Business Question
> Is technology helping the business grow while reducing long-term risk?
## Desired Outcome
A mature technology strategy capability that supports secure, reliable business operations.
## Assessment Philosophy
Assess conversationally, remain vendor agnostic, and focus on business outcomes.
