# Assessment Invitation Email

## Purpose

Invite a prospective or existing customer to complete a StackScore Technology Maturity Assessment.

This email serves as the customer's introduction to StackScore and should clearly communicate the value of participating in the assessment.

Unlike the Account Activation email, this message is invitation-based and may be sent before a purchase has been made.

---

# Trigger

Sent when an administrator selects **Invite Client to Assessment** from within StackScore.

Possible scenarios include:

- New sales opportunity
- Existing managed services customer
- Quarterly reassessment
- Partner referral
- Internal business assessment
- Marketing campaign
