# Quarterly Technology Review Invitation

## Purpose

Invite customers to schedule a Quarterly Technology Review (QTR) to review their environment, progress, and roadmap.

This email reinforces StackScore as a living platform rather than a one-time assessment.

---

# Trigger

Sent every 90 days after a customer completes their Technology Maturity Assessment.

---

# Subject

It's Time for Your Quarterly Technology Review

---

# Preview Text

Let's review what's changed in your technology environment.

---

# Customer Journey

Quarter Ends

↓

↓

↓

Roadmap Updated

# Primary Goal

Encourage the customer to book a Quarterly Technology Review.
