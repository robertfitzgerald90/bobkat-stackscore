# Account Activation Email

## Purpose

The Account Activation email is the first customer touchpoint after purchasing a StackScore Technology Maturity Assessment.

Its purpose is to welcome the customer, reinforce the value of their purchase, and guide them into activating their account so they can begin the assessment.

This email should create confidence that the customer has invested in a premium consulting platform.

---

# Trigger

Sent immediately after:

- Successful Stripe payment
- Customer account creation
- Activation token generation

---

# Subject

Welcome to StackScore — Activate Your Technology Assessment

---

# Preview Text

Activate your account and begin discovering your organization's technology maturity.
