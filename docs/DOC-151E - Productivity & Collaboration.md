# DOC-151E – Productivity & Collaboration
## Purpose
This document defines the Productivity & Collaboration assessment standard within the StackScore Technology Maturity Framework.
## Business Question
> Does technology enable employees to work efficiently and securely?
## Desired Outcome
A mature productivity & collaboration capability that supports secure, reliable business operations.
## Assessment Philosophy
Assess conversationally, remain vendor agnostic, and focus on business outcomes.
