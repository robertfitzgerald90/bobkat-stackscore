# Purpose

The Service Catalog defines every professional service offered by BobKat IT.

It serves as the operational foundation for the Technology Roadmap, Proposal Engine, Project Generation, and future Billing Engine.

The Service Catalog does **not** define pricing.

Pricing is managed separately through the Pricing Engine Specification (DOC-102).

---

# Objectives

The Service Catalog exists to:

* Standardize BobKat IT service offerings.
* Eliminate inconsistent proposal creation.
* Connect StackScore findings to professional services.
* Enable reusable service bundles.
* Generate projects automatically.
* Maintain a consistent consulting methodology.

---

# Guiding Principles

## Business Outcomes Before Technology

Services represent business outcomes rather than hardware.

Preferred:

* Firewall Modernization
* Business Continuity
* Wireless Infrastructure Deployment

Avoid:

* Install UniFi Dream Machine Pro
* Replace Cisco Switch

Technology recommendations belong in the Approved Technology Catalog.

---

## Standardized Delivery

Each service should have a clearly defined scope.

Every consultant should deliver the service consistently regardless of who performs the work.

---

## Modular Design

Services should remain independent whenever possible.

Multiple services may be combined into a Service Bundle.

---

## Assessment Driven

Services should originate from Improvement Opportunities generated by StackScore.

Clients should not normally receive proposals without first completing a Technology Profile.

---

# Service Categories

The Service Catalog is organized into six categories.

---

## 1. Assess & Plan

Purpose

Develop the client's Technology Profile and long-term technology strategy.

Services

* StackScore Technology Assessment
* Quarterly Technology Review
* Technology Roadmap Planning
* Virtual CIO (vCIO) Strategy Session

---

## 2. Modernize

Purpose

Improve technology reliability, performance, scalability, and lifecycle management.

Services

* Firewall Modernization
* Network Infrastructure Deployment
* Wireless Infrastructure Deployment
* Server Deployment & Migration
* Rack Organization
* UPS Deployment
* Internet Circuit Coordination
* Structured Cabling Coordination

---

## 3. Secure

Purpose

Reduce cybersecurity risk and improve organizational resilience.

Services

* Managed Endpoint Security
* Microsoft 365 Security Hardening
* Multi-Factor Authentication Deployment
* Email Security
* Vulnerability Management
* Security Awareness Training

---

## 4. Protect

Purpose

Protect business data and ensure operational continuity.

Services

* Backup & Disaster Recovery Deployment
* Microsoft 365 Backup
* NAS Backup Deployment
* Disaster Recovery Planning
* Backup Validation
* Disaster Recovery Testing

---

## 5. Manage

Purpose

Provide ongoing operational support and technology lifecycle management.

Services

* Managed IT Services
* Remote Monitoring & Management
* Patch Management
* Asset Lifecycle Management
* Documentation Management
* Quarterly StackScore Reviews

---

## 6. Residential

Purpose

Provide structured residential technology services using the same professional methodology as commercial engagements.

Services

* New Computer Setup
* Home Wi-Fi Optimization
* Smart Home Networking
* Home Office Setup
* Printer Installation
* Data Migration
* Virus Removal
* NAS Installation
* Technology Consultation

# Service Definition

Every service shall include the following attributes.

## General

* Service Name
* Category
* Business Outcome
* Executive Description
* Technical Description
* Active Status

---

## Delivery

* Estimated Duration
* Complexity
* Deliverables
* Exclusions
* Required Skills
* Default Project Template

---

## StackScore Integration

* Related Improvement Opportunities
* Technology Categories Improved
* Expected Technology Profile Impact
* Suggested Priority

---

## Proposal Integration

* Executive Summary Text
* Proposal Description
* Suggested Bundle Membership

---

## Administrative

* Internal Notes
* Version
* Date Created
* Last Updated

# Service Bundles

Services may belong to one or more Service Bundles.

Examples include:

Business

* Technology Foundation
* Infrastructure Modernization
* Cybersecurity Essentials
* Business Continuity
* Managed IT Onboarding
* Microsoft 365 Secure

Residential

* New Computer Setup
* Home Office Package
* Whole Home Wi-Fi
* Technology Tune-Up
* Move-In Technology Package

Bundles should remain customizable during proposal creation.

# Business Rules

* Every service shall belong to exactly one primary category.
* Services may belong to multiple bundles.
* Every service shall produce a measurable business outcome.
* Every service shall map to one or more Improvement Opportunities.
* Services shall remain vendor-agnostic.
* Services shall never directly reference hardware models.
* Services shall support automatic project generation.

---

# Future Considerations

Future versions of the Service Catalog may support:

* Service versioning
* Estimated ROI calculations
* AI-assisted service recommendations
* Industry-specific service templates
* Regional service variations
* Multi-consultant delivery templates

---

# Acceptance Criteria

The Service Catalog Specification is considered successful when:

* Every BobKat IT offering exists as a standardized service.
* Assessment findings consistently map to services.
* Services can be reused across multiple proposals.
* Services generate standardized projects.
* Proposal generation requires minimal manual effort.
* New services can be introduced without redesigning the platform.
