# DOC-151A – Identity & Access
## Purpose
This document defines the Identity & Access assessment standard within the StackScore Technology Maturity Framework.
## Business Question
> Can we trust that only the right people have access to our systems and data?
## Desired Outcome
A mature identity & access capability that supports secure, reliable business operations.
## Assessment Philosophy
Assess conversationally, remain vendor agnostic, and focus on business outcomes.
