# 1. Executive Summary

## Overview

The StackScore Communications Platform provides the centralized communication engine for the StackScore ecosystem.

Rather than functioning as a simple email delivery system, the Communications Platform manages the complete customer communication lifecycle—from the first assessment invitation through long-term customer engagement.

The platform enables Bobkat IT to communicate consistently, professionally, and automatically while measuring customer engagement throughout every stage of the consulting process.

Every communication generated by StackScore should reinforce the platform's professionalism, strengthen customer trust, and guide organizations toward improving their technology maturity.

---

## Purpose

The Communications Platform exists to accomplish four primary goals:

1. Deliver professional, branded customer communications.
2. Increase customer engagement through automated communication workflows.
3. Measure customer engagement through meaningful analytics.
4. Maintain a complete historical record of every customer interaction.

The platform should eliminate fragmented communication tools and provide a single source of truth for customer engagement.

---

## Business Value

Technology consulting is built upon relationships.

Every interaction with a customer influences how they perceive Bobkat IT and StackScore.

Professional communication increases customer confidence, improves assessment completion rates, encourages roadmap adoption, and strengthens long-term managed service relationships.

Rather than simply notifying customers that "something happened," each communication should educate, build trust, and clearly explain the next step in the customer's technology journey.

The Communications Platform transforms communication from an administrative task into a strategic business asset.

---

## Strategic Goals

The Communications Platform should:

- Improve customer onboarding.
- Increase assessment completion rates.
- Encourage roadmap adoption.
- Increase proposal acceptance.
- Improve project transparency.
- Strengthen long-term customer relationships.
- Provide measurable communication analytics.
- Support repeatable consulting workflows.
- Reduce manual administrative effort.

---

## Vision Statement

StackScore should provide one of the most polished customer communication experiences in the technology consulting industry.

Every email, notification, and customer interaction should reflect the professionalism expected from a premium consulting platform.

Customers should never feel they are interacting with a collection of disconnected emails or automated notifications.

Instead, every communication should feel intentional, valuable, and connected to their broader technology improvement journey.

---

## Product Position

The Communications Platform is not an email system.

It is a customer engagement platform.

Email is simply the first communication channel supported.

Future communication channels may include:

- SMS
- Microsoft Teams
- Slack
- Push Notifications
- In-Application Notifications
- Webhooks
- CRM Integrations

The underlying architecture should remain channel-agnostic.

---

## Relationship to StackScore

The Communications Platform serves every major StackScore module.

Examples include:

- Technology Assessments
- Recommendations
- Roadmaps
- Projects
- Technology Catalog
- Client Portal
- Proposal Engine
- Future Automation Engine

Every major module should be capable of generating communications through a centralized communication service rather than implementing its own email logic.

---

## Core Responsibilities

The Communications Platform is responsible for:

- Email template management
- Branded email rendering

- Campaign management

---

## Design Philosophy

The Communications Platform should follow the same principles established throughout StackScore:

- Professional over flashy
- Useful over noisy
- Automated where appropriate
- Transparent by default
- Measurable whenever possible
- Consistent across every customer interaction

Every communication should answer three questions for the customer:

1. What happened?
2. Why does it matter?
3. What should I do next?

If an email cannot clearly answer those questions, it should be redesigned.

---

## Success Definition

The Communications Platform will be considered successful when it enables Bobkat IT to:

- Send every customer communication from StackScore.
- Preview every communication without production workflows.
- Track customer engagement from invitation through project completion.
- Measure communication effectiveness.
- Improve consulting workflow efficiency.
- Strengthen the overall customer experience.

Ultimately, the Communications Platform should become the operational communication hub for every customer interaction within StackScore.

# 2. Customer Lifecycle Model

## Overview

Every organization interacting with StackScore follows a technology improvement journey.

The Communications Platform exists to guide customers through that journey using timely, relevant, and valuable communications rather than isolated transactional emails.

Each stage of the lifecycle represents an opportunity to educate the customer, reinforce trust, encourage progress, and strengthen the relationship between the customer, StackScore, and Bobkat IT.

Rather than viewing emails as independent events, StackScore should treat communications as part of an ongoing customer experience.

---

# Lifecycle Philosophy

Technology consulting is not a single engagement.

Organizations continually evolve.

Their technology changes.

Business priorities shift.

New risks emerge.

The Communications Platform should encourage customers to continuously evaluate and improve their technology environment rather than treating assessments as one-time events.

Every communication should move the customer toward the next stage of technology maturity.

# 3. Product Architecture

## Overview

The Communications Platform is a core service within StackScore responsible for generating, delivering, tracking, and analyzing customer communications.

Rather than allowing each module to independently send emails or notifications, all communications should pass through a centralized platform that provides consistent branding, reusable templates, historical tracking, analytics, and future multi-channel delivery.

The Communications Platform should function as a shared service that every StackScore module can leverage.

---

# Architectural Philosophy

The Communications Platform should be designed using a service-oriented architecture.

Individual StackScore modules should never be responsible for generating customer-facing communications directly.

Instead, each module should generate an event that is processed by the Communications Platform.

Example:

Assessment Completed

↓

Communications Platform

↓

Generate Assessment Complete Email

↓

Send Email

↓

Record History

↓

Update Analytics

↓

Create Timeline Event

This approach ensures consistency across the application while reducing duplicated logic.

---

# Core Responsibilities

The Communications Platform is responsible for:

- Rendering email templates
- Delivering communications
- Managing campaigns
- Recording communication history
- Tracking delivery events
- Tracking customer engagement
- Maintaining communication analytics
- Updating client timelines
- Supporting future communication channels

The Communications Platform is **not** responsible for business logic belonging to assessments, projects, proposals, or authentication.

Instead, it reacts to business events generated elsewhere within StackScore.

# 5.1 Communications Dashboard

The Communications Dashboard serves as the operational command center for the StackScore Communications Platform.

The dashboard should answer four primary questions:

# 5.2 Email Template Library

## Overview

The Email Template Library serves as the centralized repository for every customer-facing communication within StackScore.

Rather than embedding email layouts and messaging throughout the application, all communications should originate from reusable, version-controlled templates managed through a single administrative interface.

The Template Library should ensure consistency across every customer interaction while allowing administrators to preview, test, and maintain communications without requiring production workflows.

The Email Template Library represents the single source of truth for all StackScore email communications.

---

# Business Objectives

The Email Template Library exists to:

- Standardize all customer communications.
- Maintain consistent branding.
- Reduce duplicated email code.
- Allow rapid iteration of messaging.
- Support reusable components.
- Enable future localization.
- Support future white-label deployments.
- Improve testing before production deployment.

---

## Administrators

- What happened next?

# 6. Data Architecture

## Overview

The Communications Platform requires a robust and extensible data model capable of supporting transactional communications, engagement tracking, customer history, campaigns, analytics, and future workflow automation.

Rather than storing isolated email records, the Communications Platform should maintain a connected data model where communications are directly associated with organizations, users, assessments, proposals, projects, campaigns, and future StackScore modules.

The architecture should prioritize:

- Normalization
- Scalability
- Traceability
- Auditability
- Performance
- Extensibility

The Communications Platform should never duplicate business data already owned by another module.

Instead, communications should reference existing records whenever possible.

---

# Design Principles

The Communications Platform should follow the following architectural principles:

## Single Source of Truth

Organizations, users, assessments, projects, and proposals should continue to own their business data.

The Communications Platform references those records.

---

## Event Driven

Every meaningful business event should create communication events rather than duplicate workflow state.

---

## Immutable History

Historical communication records should never be deleted.

Instead they should be archived.

Audit history should remain permanently available.

---

## Provider Agnostic

The architecture should support multiple communication providers without requiring schema redesign.

---

## Future Ready

The schema should support future communication channels including:

- Email
- SMS
- Microsoft Teams
- Slack
- Push Notifications
- Webhooks

---

# 8. Security & Permissions

## Overview

The Communications Platform must enforce the same security principles established throughout the StackScore platform while ensuring that sensitive customer communications remain accessible only to authorized users.

Every communication, campaign, timeline event, and analytical report should respect the application's authorization model and organizational boundaries.

Security should be considered a foundational architectural component rather than an optional enhancement.

---

# Security Philosophy

The Communications Platform should follow four guiding principles.

## Least Privilege

Users should receive only the permissions required to perform their responsibilities.

No user should receive administrative access unless explicitly assigned.

---

## Organization Isolation

Organizations must remain completely isolated from one another.

Users should never have visibility into communications belonging to another organization unless explicitly authorized.

Multi-tenant isolation is mandatory.

---

## Auditability

Every administrative action should generate an audit record.

Examples include:

- Campaign Created
- Campaign Deleted
- Template Updated
- Test Email Sent
- Invitation Cancelled
- Communication Resent
- Automation Disabled

Audit records should be immutable.

---

## Secure by Default

New features should default to the most restrictive permissions.

Access must be explicitly granted rather than implicitly allowed.

---

# User Roles

- Manage templates
- Create campaigns
- View analytics

- Send test emails

Responsible for assigned customer organizations.

- Send invitations
- Create campaigns
- View assigned communications
- View assigned analytics
- View client timelines
- Send follow-up communications

Consultants should not modify platform templates.

## Business Owner

Executive visibility across the platform.

Capabilities include:

- View all communications
- View analytics
- View campaign performance
- Review customer engagement
- Export reports

Business Owners may not modify technical platform configuration unless granted administrative privileges.

---

## Client User

Customer portal access only.

Capabilities include:

- View customer-facing communications
- View roadmap notifications
- View proposal notifications
- View project notifications
- View customer-visible timeline events

Client users should never access administrative communications.

The Communications Platform is designed to evolve alongside StackScore as the platform matures from a Technology Maturity Assessment solution into a comprehensive operating system for technology consulting.

The initial implementation focuses on professional email communications, customer engagement, and operational visibility.

Future releases will expand the platform into a complete customer engagement ecosystem supporting consulting, managed services, technology lifecycle management, and business intelligence.

Every enhancement should reinforce StackScore's core philosophy:

**Reduce complexity. Increase capability.**

The Communications Platform should become the central nervous system connecting customers, consultants, technology, projects, and business operations.

---

# Product Vision

The long-term vision is to create the most complete communications and customer engagement platform available for technology consultants and Managed Service Providers.

Rather than relying on disconnected systems for:

- Email
- CRM
- Marketing
- Customer Success
- Reporting
- Notifications

StackScore should provide a unified experience where customer engagement is directly connected to the customer's technology journey.

Communications should become a strategic advantage rather than an operational necessity.

---

# Product Maturity Model

The Communications Platform should evolve through clearly defined maturity phases.

## Phase 1 — Foundation

Objective

Establish a centralized communications platform.

Primary Features

- Email Templates
- Shared Components
- Email Preview
- Test Emails
- Assessment Invitations
- Campaigns
- Communication History
- Delivery Tracking
- Analytics
- Client Timeline

Primary Outcome

Professional and measurable customer communication.

---

## Phase 2 — Customer Engagement

Objective

Increase customer interaction.

Planned Features

- Reminder Workflows
- Quarterly Review Scheduling
- Personalized Communications
- Customer Segmentation
- Engagement Scoring
- Consultant Dashboards
- Follow-up Recommendations
- Improved Analytics

Primary Outcome

Customers remain continuously engaged.

---

## Phase 3 — Workflow Automation

Objective

Reduce repetitive consulting work.

Planned Features

- Visual Workflow Builder
- Delayed Actions
- Conditional Logic
- Approval Workflows
- Intelligent Follow-ups
- Event Chaining
- Automation Templates

Primary Outcome

Routine consulting workflows become automated while remaining fully auditable.

---

## Phase 4 — Multi-Channel Communication

Objective

Expand beyond email.

Planned Features

- SMS
- Microsoft Teams
- Slack
- Push Notifications
- In-App Notifications
- Mobile Notifications
- Webhooks

Primary Outcome

Customers receive communications through their preferred channels.

---

## Phase 5 — Customer Success Platform

Objective

Transition from communication management to customer success management.

Planned Features

- Customer Health Scores
- Executive Business Reviews
- Customer Success Playbooks
- Renewal Management
- Success Plans
- Customer Risk Monitoring
- Engagement Recommendations

Primary Outcome

StackScore actively helps consultants retain and grow customer relationships.

---

## Phase 6 — Business Intelligence

Objective

Transform Communications Analytics into executive business intelligence.

Planned Features

- Executive Dashboards
- Revenue Attribution
- Customer Lifetime Value
- Technology Adoption Metrics
- Industry Benchmarking
- Predictive Analytics
- AI-generated Insights
- Executive Reporting

Primary Outcome

Business leaders gain complete visibility into consulting performance and customer engagement.
