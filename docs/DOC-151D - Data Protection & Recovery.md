# DOC-151D – Data Protection & Recovery
## Purpose
This document defines the Data Protection & Recovery assessment standard within the StackScore Technology Maturity Framework.
## Business Question
> Could the business recover from data loss, ransomware, or a major outage?
## Desired Outcome
A mature data protection & recovery capability that supports secure, reliable business operations.
## Assessment Philosophy
Assess conversationally, remain vendor agnostic, and focus on business outcomes.
